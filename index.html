About

Skills Not Included is a gaming sub division of The Destruction Network. We strive to bring the feeling of a community to our viewers and friends on Twitch, Discord and all over the internet. Whether you're here to watch someone build something amazing, drawing something breath-taking, or just plain destroying the opposition, we hope to bring you solid content and entertainment for a long time to come. So, please, come in, join discussions, and enjoy! Just remember, play hard and skills are definitely not included!

Membership is now open

The following is a set of guidelines that we have put together for the SNI Community.

  1. Always use manners no matter what!
  2. Always use Twitch etiquette in other peoples channel.
  3. All Games & Categories Allowed.
  4. #SNI in Stream Title and/or Twitter is always Welcome.
  5. Support Other #SNI Streamers By doing one of the following.
    • Viewing - Following - Raid/Hosting
    • Positive Encouragement
    • Be Sure to Link the Community Page: #SNI
